✅ 1. Market Data & Aggregators

**CoinGecko**
Global cryptocurrency market data aggregator with trending coins and categories
- GlobalMetrics: Global crypto market data including market cap, volume, and dominance
- TrendingCoins: Trending and most searched cryptocurrencies
- CoinCategories: DeFi, NFT, and Exchange token categories
- ExchangeData: Exchange volumes and trust scores

---

✅ 2. On-Chain Analytics

**Nansen**
On-chain analytics platform for smart money tracking and wallet intelligence
- SmartMoneyInsights: Wallet labels and smart money tracking
- TokenGodMode: Token holder distribution and flow analysis
- NFTAnalytics: NFT wallet and collection tracking
- WalletTracking: Individual wallet activity monitoring

---

✅ 3. DeFi Analytics

**DefiLlama**
DeFi protocol TVL aggregator and yield tracker
- TVLStatistics: Protocol and chain TVL data
- YieldFarming: APY and yield farming opportunities
- StablecoinMetrics: Stablecoin supply and dominance tracking
- ProtocolRevenue: Protocol fees and revenue metrics

---

✅ 4. Trading & Derivatives Platforms

**Coinalyze**
Multi-exchange derivatives analytics platform with CVD and open interest tracking
- CVDAnalysis: Cumulative volume delta analysis
- OpenInterestTracking: Multi-exchange open interest aggregation
- SwapOperations: Perpetual swap analytics
- FundingRateAnalysis: Cross-exchange funding rate comparison

---

✅ 5. DEX + AMM Data Sources

**Uniswap Subgraph / API**
Uniswap DEX data including pools, positions, and swaps
- PoolAnalytics: Pool TVL, volume, and fees
- SwapExecutor: Swap transaction data
- PositionManager: LP position tracking
- TickData: Concentrated liquidity tick ranges

---

✅ 6. NFT + Social + Sentiment

**Dune Analytics**
Custom SQL queries and on-chain analytics dashboards
- CustomQueries: SQL query execution engine
- DashboardData: Pre-built dashboard metrics
- NFTTracking: NFT collection analytics
- ProtocolMetrics: Custom protocol analytics

---

✅ 7. News & Research

**Messari**
Cryptocurrency research platform with institutional-grade data
- ResearchReports: Project research and analysis reports
- MarketData: Institutional-grade market data
- TokenProfiles: Detailed token information and profiles
- IndustryMetrics: Sector and industry analytics
